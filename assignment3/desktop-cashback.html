Cash Back

As exactly how it sounds like. You get cashback every time you swipe or use your credit card. Typically, Cash Back is more widely used by everyone as it is the easiest to redeem, and most credit card offers 1% to 5% back on certain purchases.

What are their purpose?

Most beginner credit cards offer cashback for their cards as it brings in more people wanting to open an account with them.

Cash Back is relatively easy to understand and use. That is why most people choose to go with cash back rather than points or miles. They also offer some incentive for using the card, which in the end, helps the issuers.

Getting some sort of value out of using the card makes everyone happy. For instance, if you spend $100 with a 5% CB. You will get $5 back. It may not seem like a lot, but with a lot of usages, the CB will continue to add. You can redeem them for statement credit or for gift cards.
